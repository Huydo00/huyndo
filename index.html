About.

Huy N. Do is currently 3rd year student in the Mechanical Engineering Department (ME) in the Mechatronics at University of Ho Chi Minh City (IUH). He studied mechatronics engineering in 2021-2025. His research focuses on the application of artificial intelligence for the analyze and optimize automated systems. Especially, his favorite research direction is designing control software PLC and embedded for automated systems, manufacturing plants, etc. Now, he is lecturer belongs to the field of internet of things, robotics at the TEKY ACADEMY
